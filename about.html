TVS APACHE RR310

  • INTRODUCTION

    • The TVS Apache RR 310 is a sport bike made by the TVS Motor Company in collaboration with BMW Motorrad that was launched in India on December 6, 2017 . It uses a 312.2 cc (19.05 cu in) single-cylinder, four-stroke, SI, liquid-cooled reverse inclined engine.
